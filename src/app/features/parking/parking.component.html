Vehicles Parking

the parking system allows parking services to be used also by the public without limiting hotel guests in any way. The system is capable of monitoring the car park occupancy and, via the display placed at the entry, it can signalize the number of vacant parking spaces or to inform drivers about the car park situation via the vacant/occupied sign. The parking system can be interconnected with the hotel payment and booking system through a universal interface.
